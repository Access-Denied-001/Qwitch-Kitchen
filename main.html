  • Caprese Salad

    Ingredients

    1. Fresh tomatoes
    2. Fresh mozzarella cheese
    3. Fresh basil leaves
    4. Extraa virgin olive oil
    5. Balsamic vinegar
    6. Salt
    7. Pepper

    Process

    1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella cheese
    2. Arrange them on a plate, alternating between tomato and cheese slices.
    3. Sprinkle with torn basil leaves.
    4. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
    5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  • Pasta Aglio e Olio

    Ingredients

    1. Spaghetti
    2. Garlic
    3. Red pepper flakes
    4. Parsley
    5. Olive oil
    6. Salt
    7. Black pepper
    8. Parmesan cheese

    Process

    1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic and red pepper flakes until fragrant.
    3. Add cooked spaghetti to the pan, toss to coat with the oil mixture.
    4. Season with salt and pepper, sprinkle with chopped parsley.
    5. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

  • Avocado Toast

    Ingredients

    1. Bread slices
    2. Ripe avocados
    3. Lemon juice
    4. Salt
    5. Black pepper
    6. Red pepper flakes
    7. Cherry tomatoes

    Process

    1. Toast bread slices until golden brown. Mash ripe avocados with lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.
    2. Spread the avocado mixture on the toasted bread.
    3. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired. Optionally, top with sliced cherry tomatoes.
